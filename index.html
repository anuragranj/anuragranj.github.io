Anurag Ranjan

Research Scientist, Apple.

I am a Research Scientist at Apple Machine Learning Research. I lead the research around Neural Rendering, 3D Generative models and Efficients Networks. Specifically, my work focuses on 3D generative models for humans and faces targetted towards Apple Vision Pro applications. I have also developed fast neural networks such as MobileOne and FastViT, which have shipped on several devices iPhone, iPad, Apple Vision Pro across several applications including Maps, Siri and Hand Tracking.
